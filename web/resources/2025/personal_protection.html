Personal Protections During Runs

Verifying the identity of the customer or recipient is another crucial step in protecting yourself. Goyerv supports identity confirmation features, and Runners should use them whenever available. Before making a handoff, confirm the recipient's name and, if agreed upon, request an ID or use the platform’s built-in confirmation tools. Never deliver a parcel to someone who cannot verify their identity, especially if the parcel contains valuables or sensitive materials.
